Case Study

6,000 members. One plugin. Every member gets Titan’s knowledge in their AI tools.

CCT0 Deployed · Running

Titan Network had the knowledge. Members couldn’t access it where they work.

Titan Network is one of the largest Amazon education companies in the world. 6,000+ members across 5 business units. The company has years of deep expertise in Amazon selling — strategies, PPC optimization, brand analytics, supplier management.

But that knowledge lived in courses, calls, and community threads. Members were already using AI tools like OpenClaw and Claude Code to run their businesses. Titan’s expertise wasn’t available where members actually work.

The challenge: take everything Titan knows, combine it with each member’s own Amazon data, and deliver it through the tools members already use. Not a new app to learn. Not a chatbot on a website. A plugin that works inside their existing workflow.

Members were using AI tools every day. Titan’s expertise wasn’t in any of them.

TitanConnect: Titan’s knowledge as a plugin for any AI tool.

X-Arc built and deployed TitanConnect — a plugin that gives every Titan member access to the company’s full knowledge base and their own Amazon seller data, directly inside the AI tools they already use.

28 AI tools. Instant setup. Works with OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex, and any MCP-compatible agent. Members connect once, and their AI tool immediately understands Titan’s strategies, their brand’s performance data, and their PPC campaigns.

No new interface to learn. No separate app. The knowledge shows up where the member is already working.

One install. 28 tools. Titan’s expertise and your Amazon data, inside the AI you already use.

What TitanConnect gives every member.

01

Titan knowledge tools

4 tools that give the AI access to Titan’s full methodology. Strategies, frameworks, PPC playbooks, sourcing guides. The AI answers with Titan’s expertise, not generic advice.

02

Amazon seller data tools

2 tools that connect the member’s Amazon account. The AI sees their actual brand data — sales, rankings, inventory. Answers grounded in real numbers, not guesses.

03

PPC and analytics tools

22 tools covering campaign performance, keyword data, spend optimization, and brand analytics. The AI can analyze their PPC and suggest specific actions based on Titan’s methodology.

04

Works everywhere

MCP + REST compatible. OpenClaw, Claude Code, Codex — any tool that supports plugins. Members don’t switch tools. TitanConnect meets them where they are.
